Project Analysis for Truffles and Trifles

By Machelle Burgin

Site Intent

The object of this redesign is to improve the IA (Information Architecture). This will allow for a better UI (User Interface). The information will be condensed from the 12 pages down to 6 pages.

The focus will be to manipulate proper labeling and sorting so that users will be able to browse and/or search more efficiently without being overwhelmed with excessive and repetitive information. Also each pages needs to provide more consistency with the sites main objective.

Competitive Analysis

After viewing the competitors sites there are a few things that need to incorporated into the Truffles and Trifles site. All the other sites were more visually appealing while Truffles and Trifles looked cluttered. The information on the site is unorganized. Each sub-page is also inconsistent with the company logo and the use of different fonts for the titles.

Targeted Audience

Alexa.com was the only site that provided information about the demographics of the average user. The average user is a female college graduate, married with children. They are local to area and looking for classes to take with their spouse. To create more traffic the website needs to continue to appeal to couples but also appeal more to groups.
